Event:  Global Leadership Meeting

Venue:  The Ritz-Carlton, Fort Lauderdale

Date:  January, 2025

Project Manager:  Nolan Wherley

Technical Director:  George Bamforth


All 3D renders made using Blender by Nolan Wherley.

VIDEO:  Absen LED Video Wall (46' x 13') 4Wall Entertainment

LIGHTING:  Portman P1 EVO Hexagon Accent Fixtures

SCENIC:  Atomic Design Modular Panal System (Hive)

STAGING:  Circular Thrust Stage (Custom Fabrication)

Design Objectives

Transform the ballroom space while maintaining accessibility and minimizing the audiovisual equipment footprint.

Seamlessly integrate the client's GLM branding (colors, logos) into lighting design, scenic elements, and fixtures.


Event Challenges

The asymmetrical ballroom layout required an off-center staging approach to maintain clear sightlines for attendees.

Structural video and lighting equipment were discreetly incorporated into scenic design elements to enhance aesthetics without compromising functionality.


Project Success

The client provided overwhelmingly positive feedback, praising our team's ability to exceed expectations while achieving all project objectives.
